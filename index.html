About INRC Token

INRC (Indian Rupee Coin) is a Polygon-based cryptocurrency designed to represent India in the decentralized financial ecosystem. It offers fast, low-cost, and scalable transactions with transparency.

Technical Specifications:

  • Blockchain: Polygon (Layer 2)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Total Supply: 21 million INRC
